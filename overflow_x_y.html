Overflow-x and overflow-y

You can also change the overflow of the content horizontally or vertically.

Overflow-x specifies what to do with the left/right edges of the content.

Overflow-y specifies what to do with the top/bottom edges of the content.

You can use the overflow property when you want to have better control of the layput. The overflow property specifies what happens if content overflows an element's box.
